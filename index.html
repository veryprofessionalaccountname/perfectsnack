our mission

Creating the next generation of snacks

Here at Project PerfectSnack, we are not satisfied until our snacks are perfect. Using the latest advancements in the world of molecular gastronomy, our goal is to create a perfect crunchy snack with a delicate cheese flavor.

Meet Our snack factory

Located in Truth or Consequences, New Mexico, our factory is capable of producing 85000 tons of crunchy goodness per year.

With 26 production lines working 24/7, our factory aims to deliver our cheese flavor to customers around the globe.

The Secret Ingredient

Decades of research have shown us that there is only one thing powerful enough to bring the taste of our snacks to the next level.
Certain components present in pineapples are a perfect match to the breathtaking cheese flavor of our crunchy snacks.
However, not every pineapple will do the job. Only the best pineapples, raised in perfect climate without the use of chemicals, are good enough to be used in our production.
